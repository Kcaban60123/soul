About Us

Soul by the Pound is the brainchild of culinary aficionado Chef Jacqui. After many years spent learning and perfecting her craft, Miss Jacqui has decided to use her expertise for a new personal endevor. To bring her culinary dishes and flavor to the public.

"I believe food should look as good as it taste. I use the finest ingredients, herbs and spices to pull off my artistic creations. I put a new spin on the old classics, as well as creating my own original dishes. Each and every recipe is served with love and care....... So come and get some!". -Miss Jacqui

What We Do

We provide high quality culinary dishes to our customers in the form of individual orders or catering orders
